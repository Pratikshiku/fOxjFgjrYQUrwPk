## Farm Product Pre-sale Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/2e2c8eb0739d49f48b6f13ec21140425/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User (Farmer), Merchant, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Agricultural Product Pre-sale
5. Agricultural Product Purchase
6. Announcement Information
7. Communication Forum
8. Rural Information
9. Message Board
10. Background Management
11. Online Consultation
12. Personal Center
13. My Posts
14. My Favorites


**Merchant Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Agricultural Product Pre-sale
5. Agricultural Product Purchase
6. Announcement Information
7. Communication Forum
8. Rural Information
9. Message Board
10. Background Management
11. Online Consultation
12. Personal Center
13. My Posts
14. My Favorites




**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Merchant Management
4. Farmer Management
5. Product Type Management
6. Agricultural Product Pre-sale Management
7. Pre-ordered Agricultural Product Management
8. Merchant Breach Management
9. Pre-sale Balance Management
10. Agricultural Product Purchase Management
11. Agricultural Product Registration Management
12. Farmer Breach Management
13. Purchase Balance Management
14. Announcement Information Management
15. Message Board
16. Communication Forum
17. System Management (Online Consultation, Carousel Management, Agricultural Information)
